Assignment 3, Game created by Chloe Han

Hey! I'm Chloe, and I'm currently a student in College of Arts and Sciences at NYU.
This is my game website, which I made to introduce the game that was made entirely by myself.
It is a simple shooting game, where you move the pointer to aim and remove gastlies that are trying to attack the village of stuffuls.
It did not take long for me to come up with this theme since I am a huge fan of pokemon.
Although it is a simple game, I'm sure you're gonna enjoy it!




How to play

  • A group of wild gastlies are approaching the village of stuffuls to attack them. Your job is to shoo them away using a weapon called "pokegun". It doesn't kill them, but it scares them away.
  • You need to aim for the gastlies and give them a shot. You can move your aim using W,A,S,D keys, and shot by pressing the space bar.
  • You can keep pressing on your space bar if you want to get rid of them more efficiently.
  • W:up A:left S:down D:right
  • You have 5 lives. If you let a gastly approach the village, you will lose one life. If you lose all 5 lives, you have failed to protect the village and so game is over.
